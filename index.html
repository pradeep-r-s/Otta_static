What is Ooota?

Ooota is a food ordering platform designed to eliminate high commission charges. We connect customers directly with restaurants, ensuring fair pricing and better profits for restaurant partners.

Whether you are a customer looking for affordable food or a restaurant aiming to grow your business, Ooota makes the process simple, transparent, and cost-effective.

How It Works

For Customers:

  • Open the Ooota app
  • Browse nearby restaurants
  • Place your order
  • Enjoy food at fair pricing

For Restaurants:

  • Register as a partner
  • List your menu
  • Receive orders directly
  • Earn more with zero commission

Why Choose Ooota?

  • Zero commission for restaurants
  • Transparent pricing for customers
  • Direct connection between users and restaurants
  • Simple and easy-to-use platform

About Us

At Ooota, we believe good food should reach people without extra burden. Unlike traditional delivery platforms, Ooota follows a Zero Commission Model. Local restaurants thrive, customers save money, and food remains authentic.

Ooota is an initiative focused on supporting local restaurants by removing heavy commission burdens. Our mission is to create a fair and sustainable food ecosystem.
